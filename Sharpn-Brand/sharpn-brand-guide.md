# Sharpn Brand Guide

## Brand
- **Name:** Sharpn
- **Tagline:** Sharpen your mind
- **Domain:** sharpn.de

## Products
- Sharpn: History (first product)
- Sharpn: Nutrition (planned)
- Sharpn: Tech (planned)
- Format: "Sharpn" in bold + ": Topic" in lighter weight

## Colors

### Primary — Nazar Blue
- **Primary:** #1A5FB4 (main brand blue — inspired by Turkish Nazar Boncuğu)
- **Light:** #E6F1FB (backgrounds, subtle fills)
- **Dark:** #0C447C (text on light backgrounds)

### Neutrals (for app UI)
- **Background Dark:** #1a1a2e (app dark theme)
- **Surface Dark:** #2d2d44 (cards, panels)
- **Text Primary:** #ffffff
- **Text Secondary:** #a0a0b0

### Accent (secondary — for product-specific highlights)
- Can vary per product line (e.g., History = warm amber, Nutrition = green, Tech = teal)

## Logo
- **Icon:** Brain with sharp point + spark at tip
- **Variants:**
  - Full logo (brain + "Sharpn" wordmark + tagline)
  - App icon (white brain on #1A5FB4 background, rounded corners)
  - Icon only (brain mark, for favicon/small uses)

## Typography
- **Wordmark:** System sans-serif, weight 500, letter-spacing -0.5px
- **Tagline:** System sans-serif, weight 400, letter-spacing 3.5px, uppercase

## Usage Notes
- Brain icon always includes the sharp point at bottom — it's the core brand metaphor
- "Sharpn" is always written with capital S, no 'e' (not "Sharpen")
- Product names: "Sharpn: History" — colon + space + topic name
